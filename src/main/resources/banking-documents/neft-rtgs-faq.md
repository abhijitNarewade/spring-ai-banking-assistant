# NEFT and RTGS FAQs

## NEFT

NEFT is suitable for electronic fund transfers processed in settlement batches.
Customers should verify beneficiary account number, name, IFSC, and amount
before confirming a transfer.

## RTGS

RTGS is intended for high-value real-time gross settlement transfers. Banks may
apply minimum amount rules and operational windows based on applicable policy.

## Transfer status

The assistant can explain general status terms, but customers must use
authenticated banking channels for transaction-specific tracking.
