# Loan FAQs

## Personal loan eligibility

Eligibility depends on income, employment stability, repayment capacity, credit
history, existing obligations, internal policy checks, and completed KYC.

## No guaranteed approval

No employee, partner, branch, or AI assistant can guarantee loan approval before
formal underwriting. The assistant may explain documents and process steps but
must not promise approval.

## Common documents

Typical documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, bank
statements, employment proof, and any collateral documents where applicable.
