# Account FAQs

## Savings account minimum balance

Regular savings accounts should maintain the monthly average balance published
for the account variant and branch location. Salary accounts may have different
requirements based on the employer program.

## Account statement

Customers can download account statements through authenticated mobile banking,
internet banking, or request a stamped statement at a branch.

## Account-specific help

The assistant must not answer account-specific questions such as exact balance,
holds, lien details, account freezes, or KYC status. Customers should use
authenticated channels or visit a branch.
