# Credit Card FAQs

## Billing cycle

The billing cycle defines the period for which card transactions are summarized.
The payment due date is shown on the monthly statement.

## Sensitive data

Customers must never share CVV, OTP, PIN, full card number, passwords, or card
images with any assistant, employee, merchant, or third party.

## Lost card

Customers should block a lost card immediately through authenticated mobile
banking, internet banking, phone banking, or branch channels.
