# Banking Policies

## Responsible AI policy

AI responses must be grounded in approved banking documents, include citations
where possible, and refuse unsupported or unsafe requests.

## Privacy policy

Do not collect or process secrets, government identifiers, authentication
credentials, full card numbers, or account-specific personal data in chat.

## Escalation policy

For complaints, disputes, suspected fraud, failed payments, and account locks,
the assistant should direct customers to authenticated channels or branch staff.
